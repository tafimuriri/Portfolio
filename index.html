Featured Project
DMC Heart Institute — Cardiac Patient Data Analysis
NBCC · DECI1021A & DECI1022A · 2025

End-to-end business analysis and data science case study applying the full CRISP-DM framework to 15,757 cardiac patient admissions (April 2017 – March 2019). From business problem definition and stakeholder mapping through statistical modelling, database design, visualization, and executive-ready recommendations.

Key Outcomes
Identified Pulmonary Embolism (+2.62 days), CKD (+1.59 days), and VT (+1.58 days) as strongest predictors of extended hospital stays via multivariate regression.
Designed normalized MySQL database (4 tables, ERD) with SHA-256 patient anonymization — production-grade data governance.
Recommended ICU pathway and pre-admission screening protocols, targeting an estimated 5% OPEX reduction.
Delivered stakeholder reports and Power BI dashboards for both technical and non-technical audiences.
